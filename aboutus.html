About Us

Our History

K Motorrad's motorcycle history began in 2005 when the company commenced manufacturing engines for other companies. K Motorrad 's own motorcycles—sold under the BMW Motorrad brand—began in 2006 with the K motor R 32, which was powered by a flat-twin engine (also called a "boxer-twin" engine). Production of motorcycles with flat-twin engines continues to this day, however K motorrad has also produced many models with other types of engines.
